Christina's Passion

Christina's goal as Mortgage Advisor & Broker is to explore the many financing options available to each client, and then work together to find the perfect fit.  Her mission is to facilitate homeownership in and around her community, so that the dreams of individuals and families can be realized-- from first homes to last!
Being a resource to the local Realtor community is also a top priority for Christina, and not just on the transactions she is a part of.   She invests her time and energy into helping Realtors grow in their own business by educating and serving as a trusted advisor.
She encourages you to explore all of the products and services her company has to offer;  and as always reach out anytime via phone, text, or email.

Meet Christina

Since 1999, Christina has been providing mortgage solutions for her clients in both Virginia and Georgia where she holds her state licenses. She has stayed in this industry for so long because she loves helping people make their own HOMES- in this way she feels like she is a valuable part of her community.
